Full OLL

OLL stands for, orient last layer. This is the third step to solving the Rubik's Cube using CFOP. CFOP is one of the fastest methods you can use to solve the Rubik's Cube. CFOP stands for, Cross, F2L (first 2 layers), OLL, and PLL (permute last layer). Knowing all of these OLL algorithms will help you to get by this step in solving the Rubik's a lot faster.
